Muffin Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup fresh blueberries

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.
  2. Stir together milk, egg, and oil in a large bowl. Add flour, baking powder, sugar, and blueberries; gently mix the batter with only a few strokes. Spoon batter into the muffin tin.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Serve hot.
